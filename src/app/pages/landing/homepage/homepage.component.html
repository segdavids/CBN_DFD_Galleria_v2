About The CBN DFD Intervention Funds

The Central Bank of Nigeria undertakes some developmental functions, to facilitate the attainment of its objectives through inclusive growth and sustainable finance. It does so in three major ways: stimulate finance to the agriculture and real sector; facilitate investment in critical infrastructure development; and deepen financial services inclusiveness.
The DFD Galleria is a compendium of pictures, video clips and disbursements to projects financed under the CBN development finance interventions to priority sectors of the economy. These interventions are in the following key sectors: Agriculture; Manufacturing; MSMEs; Export; Health; Services; and Energy / Infrastructure. This has increased lending to these sectors, encouraged exports, and conserved Nigeria’s foreign exchange reserves.
