Policy On Production & Productivity

  • To boost production and productivity, & reverse the nation's over- reliance in imports, by creating an ecosystem that targets & supports the right companies and projects with potential to immediately transform & jumpstart the productive base of the economy.
  • The objectives of the programme include the following:
  • Increase local production & productivity
  • Increase non-oil exports
  • Reduce import of certain commodities
  • Improve foreign exchange earning capacity of the economy.
RSSF-DCCR

Real Sector Support Facility

  • Established in 2018
  • To increase the flow of credit to the real sector of the economy in order to consolidate and sustain economic recovery.
  • Incentivize DMBs to direct affordable, long-term bank credit to the manufacturing, agriculture, as well other sectors considered by the CBN as employment and growth stimulating.
